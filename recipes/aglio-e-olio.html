Aglio e Olio

Description

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb dried bucatini pasta
  • 1/2 cup thin sliced garlic
  • 1 fresh lemon juiced
  • 1 cup parsley rough chopped
  • 1/4 cup red pepper pepper flake
  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring 1 large pot of salted water to boil.
  2. Add pasta to boiling water and cook until al dente.
  3. Add olive oil to a deep saute pan and heat until shimmering.
  4. Add garlic and red pepper flake to heated oil, fry for 1 minute or until garlic begins to brown.
  5. Remove from heat, add pasta and 1 cup of pasta water.
  6. Stir and add parsley, lemon juice, and salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve immediately!
