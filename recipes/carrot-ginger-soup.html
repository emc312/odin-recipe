Carrot Ginger Soup

Description

Ingredients

  • 3 large carrots peeled and diced
  • 1 small white onion diced
  • 2 celery stalks diced
  • 1/4 cup ginger peeled and diced
  • 6 cups chicken stock
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup chives chopped for garnish
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Fine dice all vegetables (carrot, onion, celery, ginger).
  2. Saute in medium sauce pot with butter and vegetable oil until carrots are soft and oinions are translucent.
  3. Deglaze pan with soy sauce, add chicken stock and simmer on low heat for 1 hour.
  4. Remove from heat and blend all ingredients in vita-mix or blender until smooth.
  5. Garnish with chopped chives and serve immediately!
